How should I store honey?

Store honey in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. A tightly sealed container will help maintain its quality. Avoid refrigeration as it can alter the texture.

What are the health benefits?

Honey is rich in antioxidants and has antibacterial properties. It may help soothe sore throats and boost immunity. Additionally, it can provide a natural energy source.

How can I use honey?

Honey can be used in tea, as a sweetener for recipes, or as a natural remedy. It also makes a great addition to smoothies and salad dressings. Experiment with it in your favorite dishes!

Is honey vegan-friendly?

Honey is produced by bees, which makes it a non-vegan product. Many vegans choose to avoid honey due to its animal origin. However, there are plant-based alternatives available.

Can honey expire?

Honey has an incredibly long shelf life and does not spoil. If stored properly, it can last indefinitely. However, it may crystallize over time, which is natural and can be reversed by gentle heating.
